Puzzle Play – Sudoku Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 May 2026

Puzzle Play – Sudoku is developed by CW Apps.

1. Personal Information

Puzzle Play – Sudoku does not require an account and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or precise location.

Gameplay progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

2. Gameplay Data

The app may store the following information locally:

  • puzzle progress
  • game statistics
  • difficulty preferences
  • background selections
  • app settings
  • premium purchase status

This data is not transmitted to the developer.

3. Advertising

The free version of Puzzle Play – Sudoku may display advertisements using Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect limited information such as device identifiers and advertising identifiers to display and measure advertisements. They may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Users in certain regions may be asked to provide consent for personalised advertising.

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage information such as app interaction events and device type. This data is used solely to improve app performance and user experience.

5. Crash Reporting

The app uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs and diagnostic data to improve stability.

6. In-App Purchases

Optional premium upgrades are available through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Payments are processed securely by Apple.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

8. Children

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated periodically.
